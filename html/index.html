Algorithms

Here's a webapp designed to help study for the final Algorithms exam. It covers asympotoic notation, divide and conquer searching and sorting, greedy and dynamic programming algorithms for optimization, and a slight amount of information about problems in P and NP.

This was created in Fall 2019 for Mississippi State University and Professor Eric Hansen.
